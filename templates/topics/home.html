About This Platform

Economics is contested terrain. Different schools of thought offer different explanations for how economies work, what causes problems like inflation and unemployment, and what policies might help.

This platform presents each perspective in its strongest form, subjects all views to the same critical scrutiny, and lets you draw your own conclusions.

Our Principles

  • Genuine pluralism - No school of thought is favored
  • Steel-man first - Present each view at its best before critique
  • Teaching, not advocacy - Help you understand, not convert you
  • Honest about limitations - Acknowledge contested evidence
